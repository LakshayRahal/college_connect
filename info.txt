Plugins in Maven enhance its functionality by enabling automation, customization, and integration with external tools in Java projects. They streamline the build process, manage dependencies, execute tasks at specific lifecycle phases, and provide flexibility to tailor the build environment according to project requirements. Understanding and effectively utilizing Maven plugins is essential for optimizing development workflows and ensuring efficient project management.
